Frequently Asked Questions

Here is where all your questions will be answered.

Purchasing

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we provide gift cards for purchase. Just ask any of our staff members, and we'll be glad to assist you!

Which name brands do you carry?

We stock a range of name brands, including Fender, Epiphone, B.C. Rich, Jay Turser, Seagull, Ovation, Oscar Schmidt, Faith, Cort, Ibanez, and several more.

Is there a return policy?

Our policy does not allow for returns. We maintain a strict no return policy.

Do you facilitate purchases and trades?

Certainly! We welcome the opportunity to examine items customers bring to our shop and negotiate deals for purchases or trades.

Renting

What types of instruments are available for rent?

We offer a variety of instruments for rent, such as clarinets, flutes, saxophones, trombones, trumpets, violins, violas, cellos, guitars, basses, and drums.

What are the rental rates?

Rental prices vary based on the instrument and the duration of the rental period.

Repairs

What kinds of instruments do you repair?

Our repair services cover a broad spectrum of needs, ranging from basic guitar setups and drum hardware repairs to more advanced tasks like repairing amp electronics and restoring band instruments. This includes addressing issues like broken keys, loose screws/slides, and more.

What is the average repair duration?

The repair timeline depends on the complexity of the repair. Simpler repairs typically take around 1-2 days, while more intricate repairs could extend up to a week.

Lessons

What is the tuition fee for lessons?

Tuition for lessons is set at $35.

Which instruments do you provide lessons for?

We offer lessons for various instruments, including but not limited to guitar, bass, drums, ukulele, piano, vocals, and music composition.

Can I expect a high-quality education for my instrument?

Absolutely. Our team consists of dedicated, experienced, and knowledgeable instructors who have specialized in their respective instruments for many years. Our educators are committed to providing effective and motivational instruction in a nurturing environment for our students.
