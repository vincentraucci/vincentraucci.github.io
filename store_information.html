Who we are

Welcome to The Guitar Shop's updated website.

We are known for carrying a broad selection of vintage guitars that are in awesome shape with unbeatable prices.

Our highly talented music instructors provide lessons from beginners to advanced for all instruments including voice.

We offer rentals for the experiment of sound, flexibility of replacement, and easy maintenance.

Come on by for a diagnoses for us to repair your instrument for better function and sound.
